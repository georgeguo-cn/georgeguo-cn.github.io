About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Min Zhang. I received my PhD degree in School of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Guohui Li.

Currently, my primary research interests include data mining and user behavior modeling, particularly in multimodal representation learning, user interests modeling and recommendation system.
